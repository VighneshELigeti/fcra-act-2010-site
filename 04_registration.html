Registration vs Prior Permission

Two legal gateways — one for organisations with a track record seeking ongoing access to foreign funding, and one for first-timers or specific one-off projects. Choosing the right route is critical to compliance.

💡 Mental Model

Think of Registration like a multi-entry visa — you get it once, it's valid for 5 years, and you can use it to receive FC from any foreign source as many times as needed.

Think of Prior Permission like a single-trip visa — it's granted for one specific journey (project), with a specific host (donor), for a specific duration. Expired when the trip is done.

The Registration Application Process

1
Prepare Documentation
Gather: registration certificate, trust deed/MOA, audited accounts for 3 years, activity reports, Aadhaar of all office bearers, PAN, bank details. All documents must be self-attested.
2
Open SBI FCRA Account
Post-2020, you must first open a designated FCRA receipt account at State Bank of India, New Delhi Main Branch (Branch code: 00691). This is a mandatory prerequisite.
3
File Online on fcraonline.nic.in
Log in with Digital Signature Certificate. Fill Form FC-3C for registration (or FC-3D for prior permission). Upload all documents. Pay prescribed fee online.
4
Intelligence Bureau Verification
MHA forwards the application to Intelligence Bureau for security clearance. IB checks the organisation, its activities, and key functionaries. This is typically the longest stage — can take 3–12 months.
5
MHA Decision
MHA grants or rejects the registration. If granted, a certificate is issued — valid for 5 years. If rejected, MHA must provide reasons and there is an appellate remedy.
6
Activate & Maintain
Once registered: maintain separate FC accounts, file annual returns by 31 December, report changes in office bearers / bank / address within 15 days, apply for renewal 6 months before expiry.

⏰ Critical Timing: Renewal Window

Registration is valid for 5 years. You must apply for renewal at least 6 months before expiry. If your registration expires and you haven't renewed, you CANNOT receive any FC, even if your renewal application is pending. Many organisations lose FCRA compliance simply by missing the renewal window. Set calendar reminders 9 months before expiry.

🚨 Post-2020 Change: Aadhaar Mandatory

The FCRA Amendment Act 2020 made Aadhaar-based KYC mandatory for all office bearers, key functionaries, and members named in the FCRA application. Foreign nationals associated with the organisation must provide their passport as the equivalent identification. This requirement applies to both new applications AND renewals. Missing Aadhaar for even one key functionary will cause the application to be rejected.
